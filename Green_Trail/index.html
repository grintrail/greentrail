About Us

In recent times, Africa has been producing some of the top talents in the world, making it one of the fast-growing continents in terms of human capital. Some Africans are born survivors, given the environment surrounding their birth, the struggles to meet ends and the desire to become a person of substance.

We are looking to capitalise on these trends by empowering young Africans with the resources (human & technological solutions) and skill-set needed to inspire the next set of entrepreneurs ready to lead the development and make it a world authority as it was in historical times with Egypt.
